Overview

The Villas at Cattail Creek is set in a serenely picturesque setting located adjacent to the Cattail Creek Country Club. Nestled in this 55 or better active adult condominium community are luxurious single family homes and attached villas. It is one level living at its finest, with great amenities and spectacular views.

The Villas at Cattail Creek is a beautiful active adult community made up of 25 single family homes built by Ryan Homes and 68 attached villas in groups of four built by NV Homes. All homes have first floor master suites, attached one or two car garages, gas heat and central air, with finished areas ranging from 2200 sq. ft. to 4700 sq. ft. You'll enjoy more free time as most exterior maintenance is done by the condominium association. The community was built between 2004 and 2006.

The Villas at Cattail Creek's amenities, low-maintenance residences, prime location, and close proximity to Cattail Creek Country Club's golf course, are ideal for today's active adult homebuyers. Look no further if you are looking for carefree living.

Some units have transferable memberships in the Cattail Creek Country Club. There are three membership tiers: dining, social (adds tennis, pool and fitness), and golf.
